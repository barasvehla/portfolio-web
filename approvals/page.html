Cutting through complexity of Approvals

About 

Approvals feature is used by marketing teams and creative agencies to get a green light on their work.

Challenge 

Users were confused by a fragmented and unclear approvals system, leading to many support tickets and poor experience.

Impact

Eliminated number of tickets for CS team. System understandable even for small teams, opening up a new market segment for the business.

TL;DR

🎫 Reduced load of support team

🧩 Unified approvals logic

🤝 Started ongoing partnership with CS team

Design process

  1. Getting data internally

I first assessed existing insights to see how much I could learn before investing into new research. Here is what I did:

  • Product audit to flag current pain points

  • Competitors' review to benchmark standards

  • Old user interviews to understand thinking behind current design

  • Review with peer designers to validate findings

From this, I created a board with a UX issues marked in it.

  1. Collaboration with Customer success team

Internal data helped me spot pain points and form early hypotheses.

To validate these, I turned to the CS team, as they are closest to the users and this project aimed to ease their workload. We mapped recurring issues, especially from enterprise clients, identified their frequency, and uncovered a few missed problems.

This gave me a more complete and evidence-based understanding of how Approvals were really used.
